About OptiPDF

Client-side PDF utilities in a small, private web app. Files stay on your device.

Open a PDF on the Home page, then use tools to merge, split, rotate, watermark, add page numbers, edit metadata, and export.

Contact options explained

  • Email app — opens your computer’s default mail program.
  • Gmail web — opens a Gmail compose window in your browser.
  • Copy email — copies the address to your clipboard so you can paste it anywhere.
